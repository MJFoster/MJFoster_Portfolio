About Me

Discussing innovative solutions and overcoming obstacles as a team, motivates me to work at my highest level of efficiency and creativity. I enjoy collaboration and embrace environments that allow me to learn from and with others. For me, developing software is the perfect combination of creative problem solving and collaboration.

For fun, I enjoying spending time with my family and friends, staying fit, golfing, and riding my horses whenever possible!

I call Boise, Idaho home, but I've lived all over central and southern California prior to 1999. I was born and raised in California, lived mostly in Santa Barbara and San Diego, and for many years enjoyed the beaches and year round sunshine.

After moving to Boise, I embraced the North West when I discovered the easily accessible outdoor playground full of activities. Those perks, coupled with its affordable cost of living and ever changing seasonal landscape, developed my appreciation of the North West and all the friendly people here!
